About Astoria

Your Partner in Hospitality Excellence

For over a decade, Astoria Hospitality has been the trusted bridge between the world's finest hospitality brands and the Gulf's most prestigious hotels and restaurants. We don't simply supply products — we curate experiences.

From the porcelain that graces a Five-Star breakfast table to the crystal that elevates a gala dinner, every piece we source reflects our commitment to uncompromising quality and timeless elegance.

With offices in Muscat and Beirut, we combine deep regional expertise with global reach — ensuring that every property we serve receives not just products, but a partnership built on trust, taste, and precision.
